Water Buddy

The Smart Way to Stay Hydrated!

Water Buddy is your personal hydration assistant, designed to help you track your water intake, monitor your water levels, and locate nearby refill stations. It seamlessly integrates with a web app for real-time hydration insights.

Key Features

Water Buddy tracks your hydration in real-time, ensuring you always know how much water you’ve consumed. It detects water levels in your bottle and helps you locate the nearest refill stations, so you never run out of water.

Unlike traditional water bottles, Water Buddy connects to a web app, eliminating the need for Bluetooth pairing or additional apps. The sleek, lightweight design makes it perfect for daily use.

How It Works

Fill your Water Buddy bottle, and it automatically detects the water level in real-time. As you drink, the built-in tracking system records your water intake and updates your hydration status on the web app.

If your water level is low, simply access the Water Buddy web app to find the closest refill stations. Designed for convenience, the system ensures you always have access to clean drinking water, whether you're at work, at the gym, or on the go.

Why Choose Water Buddy?

Proper hydration is essential for maintaining focus, energy levels, and overall well-being. Water Buddy removes the guesswork by tracking your intake and helping you meet your hydration goals effortlessly.

With built-in water level detection and a refill station locator, Water Buddy ensures that you never find yourself without water when you need it most. Whether you’re at home, work, or traveling, it’s designed to keep you hydrated at all times.
